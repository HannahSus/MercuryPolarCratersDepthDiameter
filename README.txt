
Software for:
Morphometry and Temperature of Simple Craters in Mercury's Northern Hemisphere: Implications for Stability of Water Ice

Date Created:
2021-08-31

Code Overviews:

compare_craters.py
-To compare crater depth/diameter measurements from individual MLA tracks and gridded Topography

dD_plots_lat_bins.py
-To compare depth/diameter measurements in distinct latitude bins

dD_plots_lat_runbins.py
-To compare depth/diameter measurements in overlapping latitude bins
-Used in study

dD_plots_lon_bins.py
-To compare depth/diameter measurements in distinct longitude bins

dD_plots_lon_runbin.py
-To compare depth/diameter measurements in overlapping longitude bins
-Used in study

dD_plots_secondaries.py
-To check the depth/diameter of identified secondaries compared to the general depth/diameter population

dD_plots.py
-To make general depth/diameter plots for the whole crater populations

find_mla_tracks_in_lat_bins.py
-To calculate density of MLA tracks in latitude bins per area in bin

radarbright_plots_lat_bins.py
-To calculate the percentage of radar-bright craters in latitude bins

radarbright_plots_lon_runbin.py
-To calculate the percentage of radar-bright craters in overlapping longitude bins

temp_plots.py
-To plot the maxT, avgT and minMaxT versus latitude (and longitude)
